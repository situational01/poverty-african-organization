Who We Are

POAK (Poverty Africa - Kenya) is a community-centered, nonprofit organization dedicated to fighting poverty in all its forms across Kenya. We work with vulnerable and marginalized communities to empower them through inclusive development initiatives, capacity building, advocacy, and partnerships.

POAK believes that sustainable poverty alleviation can only be achieved through collective action that brings together communities, civil society, the private sector, and government actors. Our approach is participatory, inclusive, and focused on long-term impact rather than short-term relief.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To alleviate all forms of poverty in Kenya and actively involve all sectors and strata of society in achieving this noble goal, thereby improving the living standards of Kenyans and communities beyond.

Our Vision

A Kenya free from poverty where every individual has equal opportunities to thrive, with empowered communities driving their own sustainable development.

Why POAK Exists

Despite numerous development efforts, many communities in Kenya continue to face persistent poverty, inequality, unemployment, limited access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.

Address Root Causes

Address the root causes of poverty rather than its symptoms in Kenyan communities

Strengthen Resilience

Strengthen community resilience and self-reliance across Kenya

Promote Inclusion

Promote inclusive development that leaves no Kenyan behind

Bridge Gaps

Bridge gaps between Kenyan communities, development partners, and government programs

Our Core Values

Equity & Inclusion

We believe every Kenyan deserves equal opportunities regardless of gender, age, disability, ethnicity, or socio-economic status.

Community Empowerment

We place Kenyan communities at the center of development, promoting ownership, participation, and self-reliance.

Integrity & Accountability

We uphold transparency, ethical conduct, and responsible use of resources in all our Kenya operations.

Partnership & Collaboration

We value working with Kenyan government, NGOs, private sector, and communities to achieve sustainable impact.

Sustainability

We promote long-term, environmentally and socially sustainable solutions to poverty in Kenya.

Innovation & Learning

Who We Serve in Kenya

POAK targets vulnerable and marginalized populations affected by poverty and social exclusion across Kenya

  • Kenyan youth, particularly unemployed and out-of-school youth
  • Kenyan women, including single mothers and women-headed households
  • Widows and orphans in Kenya
  • Kenyans with disabilities
  • Unemployed and underemployed Kenyans
  • Low-income households and informal settlements in Kenya
  • Rural and underserved Kenyan communities
  • Kenyan communities affected by environmental degradation and climate change
