Who We Are

Poverty African Organization (PAO) is a community-centered, nonprofit organization dedicated to fighting poverty in all its forms. We work with vulnerable and marginalized communities to empower them through inclusive development initiatives, capacity building, advocacy, and partnerships.

PAO believes that sustainable poverty alleviation can only be achieved through collective action that brings together communities, civil society, the private sector, and government actors. Our approach is participatory, inclusive, and focused on long-term impact rather than short-term relief.

Our Mission

To alleviate all forms of poverty and actively involve all sectors and strata of society in achieving this noble goal, thereby improving the living standards of Kenyans and communities beyond.

Our Vision

Target Population

PAO targets vulnerable and marginalized populations affected by poverty and social exclusion in Kenya and across Africa

  • Youth, particularly unemployed and out-of-school youth
  • Women, including single mothers and women-headed households
  • Widows and orphans
  • Persons with disabilities
  • Unemployed and underemployed individuals
  • Low-income households and informal settlements
  • Rural and underserved communities
  • Communities affected by environmental degradation and climate change
